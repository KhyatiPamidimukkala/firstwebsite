Interesting Facts

Octopuses are fascinating sea creatures known for their intelligence and problem-solving abilities. They have three hearts, blue blood, and can change both color and texture to camouflage themselves.

The Eiffel Tower in Paris was once criticized by many artists and writers when it was first built in 1889. Today, it is one of the most famous landmarks in the world and can even grow taller in summer because metal expands in heat.
